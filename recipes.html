Recipe #1:

Crispy Mozzarella

Ingredients

To make 24 pieces:


For the fry:

Preparation

Details

  • Cost: Low
  • Difficulty: Low
  • Preparation Time: 25 min
  • Cooking Time: 30 min
  • Servings: 6 people

Preparation

Crispy Mozzarella are a mouthwatering appetizer to which you will not be able to resist! With a crunchy outside and a melting heart, you can never get enough!!

To prepare the crispy mozzarella:

  1. Start by cutting the mozzarella into cubes of roughly 2 cm each.
  2. Pan and dry the mozzarella with paper towel or alternatively you can use blotting paper (two layers, one to lay and one to cover the mozzarella cubes).
  3. Press gently with your hands to remove any water in excess and set aside the mozzarella cubes.
  4. Prepare a bowl with flour, in another beat the eggs and add salt and pepper to your taste.
  5. Prepare a third bowl the breadcrumbs, add seasoning of your choice (you can use Italian herbs or pepper).
  6. Coat each mozzarella cube in flour first, then the eggs and finally in the breadcrumbs.
  7. Heat the sunflower seed oil to optimal temperature, you can check if the oil is hot enough by using the bottom end of a wooden spoon. If it sizzles, the oil is at the correct temperature.
  8. Dip the coated mozzarella in the oil and cook for a couple of minutes until golden.
  9. Drain with a slotted spoon and place the mozzarella into a plate covered with absorbent paper to avoid excess of oil.
  10. Enjoy while still hot.

Recipe Tips:

You can accompany the mozzarella with dipping sauces for an even tastier appetizer. The breaded mozzarella can be frozen and fried at need. Use baby mozzarella for a perfect size bite.

Zucchini & Scamorza

Ingredients

To make 6 Patties:


For the Breading:

Preparation

Details

  • Cost: Medium
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Preparation Time: 40 min
  • Cooking Time: 30 min
  • Servings: 8 people

Preparation

Zucchini and Scamorza patties are the perfect combination for a well-balanced vegetarian dish. The strong taste of the Scamorza will be marry the sweetness of the zucchini for a summery appetizer that will leave you breathless.

To prepare the Zucchini and Scamorza patties:

  1. Wash the zucchini, remove the ends at each side and grate with a medium-hole grater.
  2. Set one zucchini aside for decoration.
  3. Place the grated zucchini on a colander and squeeze to remove excess water.
  4. Grate the scamorza cheese with a large-hole grater.
  5. Mix the cheese and the zucchini, adding salt and paper to your like, then stir and add the breadcrumbs and a pinch of nutmeg.
  6. Finally add the grated Grana Padano cheese.
  7. Work with your hands to blend the ingredients well.
  8. Prepare 8 squares of parchment paper in a tray.
  9. Place 100gr of the mix in a food ring and model to a circular shape (patties) over each of the parchment square paper.
  10. Remove carefully the ring to maintain the shape.
  11. Place the tray in the fridge for an hour.
  12. In a bowl beat lightly the eggs, prepare the breadcrumbs lightly seasoning to your taste.
  13. Coat the patties in the eggs first and then breadcrumbs.
  14. Heat the oil at required temperature and fry the patties for 3 minutes or until golden on both side and place on top of absorbent paper to avoid excess of oil.
  15. Enjoy the patties while piping hot!

For the decoration (optional):

  1. Cut the remaining zucchini into thin slices.
  2. Roast lightly.
  3. Wrap the patties with the roasted zucchini and season to your taste.

Recipe Tips:

You can substitute the Scamorza cheese with any strong taste cheese of your like. It is always better to prepare and consume the patties immediately. No suitable for freezing.

Samosa

Ingredients

To make 8 Dough:


For the Stuffing:

Preparation

Details

  • Cost: Medium
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Preparation Time: 30 min
  • Cooking Time: 35 min
  • Servings: 4 people

Preparation

Crispy vegetable Samosa are the perfect appetizer or side dish to accompany your Indian food. They can also make the perfect addition to your buffet party with family and friends!!

To prepare the filling:

  1. Wash, peel and cut the vegetables as required.
  2. Boil the potatoes until soft and transfer them to a bowl and mash coarse.
  3. Heat some oil in a pan, mix the spices and the onion and fry until golden.
  4. Add the potatoes and the remaining vegetables, seasoning to taste and stir until cooked.
  5. Leave to cool.

For the pastry:

  1. Arrange the flour as a cone leaving a well in the centre.
  2. Add the water, the seed oil and a pinch of salt.
  3. Work with your hand until you have a smooth dough (roughly for 5-10 minutes).
  4. Roll into a ball, cover with cling film and leave it rest in a tray for 30 minutes.
  5. Once ready, divide the pastry into 8 rounding pieces.
  6. For each of those, cut in half and work the dough with your hands and a little bit of water.
  7. Fill the bottom part with the vegetable mix, using the second half to close up shaping into a cone.
  8. Repeat again for the remaining parts.
  9. Heat the vegetable oil to the require temperature for deep frying.
  10. Deep fry the samosa until crisp and brown.
  11. Place on absorbent paper to remove excess oil.
  12. Enjoy hot.

Recipe Tips:

You can substitute the vegetable filling with beef mince for meat lovers. Dip your samosa in Chutney for extra flavors.

Green Cannelloni

Preparation

Details

  • Cost: Medium
  • Difficulty: Low
  • Preparation Time: 30 min
  • Cooking Time: 45 min
  • Servings: 4 people

Preparation

Green Cannelloni, or also known as “Cannelloni Ricotta and Spinach” is a classic Italian Recipe that can bring your family together for a yummy Sunday lunch. This recipe substitutes the traditional bechamel with a tomatoes sauce giving your dishes an irresistible taste and lightness. The success of your meal is guaranteed!!!

To prepare the Cannelloni:

  1. Wash your spinach thoroughly.
  2. Drain well and stir fry in a pan with oil and garlic. Season to your liking.
  3. Once done leave it to rest.
  4. In a small pot, sauté the onion with some olive oil, add the tomatoes sauce, salt and pepper and let it cook until ready.
  5. Mix the Spinach with the Ricotta Cheese and the egg until smooth. Add salt and pepper and a pitch of nutmeg if you like it.
  6. Open the cannelloni package and fill each one with the mix or ricotta and spinach with a table spoon.
  7. Place each filled cannelloni in a tray and cover with the tomatoes sauce and mozzarella.
  8. Place the fresh basil as decoration on top.
  9. Bake for 40 minutes at 220°.
  10. Enjoy while still hot.

Recipe Tips:

You can substitute the spinach with different types of leave and add grated Parmesan Cheese to the filling for extra taste! The cannelloni can be saved in the fridge for 1 to 2 days.

Preparation

Details

  • Difficulty: Low
  • Preparation Time: 15 min
  • Cooking Time: 15 min
  • Servings: 4 people

Preparation

If you are in a hurry but still looking for something to melt your taste buds then Gnocchi with pesto is the right choice for you. This is a delicious Italian main, easy to make which will bring you to a carb heaven.

To prepare the Pesto:

  1. Unpack and wash the basil leaves thoroughly.
  2. Put the basil, in a blender or mini food processor and whirl until chopped.
  3. Add the grated Parmesan-Reggiano cheese, the garlic, the pine nuts and the olive oil and blend until smoothed.
  4. Add seasoning to your taste and extra olive oil if too dry, continue whirling until well blended.
  5. Cover and refrigerate if not used immediately.

For the gnocchi:

  1. Boil a large pot of water.
  2. When boiling add salt to your taste.
  3. Unpack the potato gnocchi and cook them following package instructions. The gnocchi will be ready when floating to the top.
  4. Drain the gnocchi.
  5. Mix the gnocchi with the pesto sauce.
  6. Add parmesan on top to your taste.

Recipe Tips:

You can mix the basil leaves with spinach and/or rucola if you want to make your pesto extra delicious! The pesto can be prepared in advanced and be frozen for future usage.

Recipe #1:

Tiramisú

Ingredients

To prepare 8 slices:

Preparation

Details

  • Cost: Low
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Preparation Time: 60 min
  • Cooking Time: 60 min
  • Servings: 4 people

Preparation

Who can resist this amazing Italian classic dessert? And what's not to like? Coffee, cream and lady fingers biscuits will bring you to another level of haven!

To prepare Tiramisú:

  1. The first step to prepare the tiramisu is to carefully separate the egg white from the yolk. It is very important to use fresh, free-range eggs for this dessert as this will be left raw.
  2. Whip carefully the yolks with an electric mixer until clear and frothy.
  3. Add the mascarpone to obtain a firm, smooth cream.
  4. Add the sugar little by little and when ready set aside.
  5. Separately beat the eggs and whisk them into stiff peaks (the mixture will be ready will not move).
  6. Add spoon by spoon to the mix of yolk, mascarpone and sugar and incorporate from bottom to top.
  7. Spread evenly a layer of the cream into a baking tray.
  8. Soak the lady finger into coffee (the coffee should be cooled down and sweetened with sugar)
  9. Place the lady fingers on top of the layer cream (all in sense).
  10. Over the first layer spread another layer of cream and smooth with a spoon.
  11. Add the cocoa powder on top and place the Tiramisu in the fridge for a few hours.
  12. Enjoy your dessert.

Recipe Tips:

You can prepare the Tiramisu and leave it overnight in the fridge. To serve spoon out on teas serving plates. The Tiramisu can be preserved in the fridge for a couple of days.

Recipe #2:

Brownie

Ingredients

To make the 20 brownies:

Preparation

Details

  • Cost: Low
  • Difficulty: Low
  • Preparation Time: 15 min
  • Cooking Time: 15 min
  • Servings: 4 people

Preparation

5 o'clock, time for tea, why not accompany it with a brownie?? This recipe will provide a fully handmade delicious way to conquer the heart of children and adult alike with a rich, fudgy in the middle, dessert.

To prepare 20 brownies:

  1. In a bowl cut small cubes of unsalted butter and mix them with finely chopped dark chocolate.
  2. Melt the mixture in the microwaves or through a water bath (e.g., place the bowl over a hot water saucepan letting the bowl sitting on the rim of the pan, place on low heat).
  3. Leave the melt mixture to cool down.
  4. Chop the white and milk chocolate into big chunks.
  5. Sift the flower and the cocoa powder together.
  6. Beat vigorously the eggs with the sugar, add the sifted powders, and the melted mixture and incorporate stirring from bottom to top.
  7. Add the chopped milk and white chocolate chunks and continue stirring until the ingredients are well mixed.
  8. Heat the oven to 180°.
  9. Line a square tray with baking paper, pour the ingredients mix, and pad the top with a spatula.
  10. Bake for 30 minutes or until the top has a dull sheen. Cool for 5 minutes, then cut into bars.
  11. Enjoy!!

Recipe Tips:

You can add any type of nuts to your brownies, serve hot with vanilla ice cream on the side for the perfect combination!

Cheesecake is a timeless dessert with many different versions being proposed. Either baked or with gelatin, cheesecakes are the perfect summer desserts you will not be able to resist!

To prepare the base:

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave for a few seconds
  2. Place the biscuits in a mixer and blend them until a fine powder is produced.
  3. Line a round tray with baking paper, making sure the borders of the try are also covered.
  4. Mix the biscuits powder with the butter and place them into the try.
  5. Pad with a spoon or a spatula covering the entire tray up to the edges.
  6. Covered and place into the fridge for at least 30 minutes.

To prepare the cream:

  1. Mix the egg and egg yolks with the sugar until is creamy,
  2. Include the vanilla
  3. Add gradually the cream cheese and whisk until fully incorporate.
  4. Add the double cream and mix well.
  5. Remove the base from the fridge and pour the cream over the base.
